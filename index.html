    <!-- Application Structure Plan: A single-page, narrative scrolling application with a sticky navigation header. This structure was chosen to guide the user through the compelling story of Mirror Space, from abstract vision to concrete execution. It starts with the core mission (the "Why"), moves to the physical space and cultural content (the "What"), details the business model (the "How"), and concludes with the future roadmap and impact. This flow is more engaging than a traditional report structure, turning a business plan into an exploratory journey. Interactive elements like tabs for the space, a dynamic chart for financials, and a clickable timeline for the roadmap encourage user engagement and deeper understanding at each stage. -->

    <!-- Visualization & Content Choices: 

        1. Report Info: Brand Spirit (反思性, 包容性, 创造性) -> Goal: Organize/Inform -> Method: Interactive Cards (HTML/CSS) -> Interaction: Hover to highlight -> Justification: Breaks up text and makes abstract concepts more tangible and visually appealing.

        2. Report Info: Functional Zones (书区, 咖啡区, etc.) -> Goal: Organize/Explore -> Method: Tabbed Interface (HTML/CSS/JS) -> Interaction: Click tabs to switch content -> Justification: Organizes detailed information into a compact, user-controlled component, simulating the exploration of a physical space.

        3. Report Info: Revenue Breakdown (收益占比) -> Goal: Compare/Inform -> Method: Doughnut Chart (Chart.js) -> Interaction: Hover over chart segments to display corresponding details in a text panel -> Justification: A doughnut chart is ideal for showing parts of a whole. The interactivity directly links quantitative data (percentages) with qualitative value propositions (在地特色价值), enhancing comprehension.

        4. Report Info: Development Plan (发展规划) -> Goal: Show Change/Process -> Method: Interactive Vertical Timeline (HTML/CSS/JS) -> Interaction: Click on a stage to view its details -> Justification: Transforms a static table into a dynamic and forward-looking narrative of growth, making the plan feel more alive and actionable. -->
